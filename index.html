Watch it!

What is Watch It?

Watch It! is a simple application to search and save movies and/or series you still need to watch.

Why?

I wanted a way to create a watch list outside of typical note applications or platforms like Netflix.

How do I use it?

Click the "Go to App" button, then you can search for a movie using the search bar, and add it to your list by clicking the "+" button.
